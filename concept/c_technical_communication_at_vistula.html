Technical communication at Vistula

Technical communication is the process of defining, creating and delivering information for the safe, efficient and effective use of products (technical systems, software, services).

Technical communication

Today, effective technical communication is more important than ever: whole industries are built around the development, retention and application of information. And the professionals who communicate complex information are technical writers. They break down complex technical products into easy-to-understand guides that help end-users understand how to use the products and services.

Technical communicators may put the information they capture into paper documents, web pages, computer-based trainings, digitally stored texts, audio, video, and other media.
